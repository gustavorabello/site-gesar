--- extends: post_en.j2 default_block: text title: GESAR group description: GESAR ---   **bus**: Many bus lines coming from the north zone, west zone and south zone stop at the tradições nordestinas center (Feira dos Paraíbas). Check your itinerary at [www.vadeonibus.com.br](http://www.vadeonibus.com.br)   **subway**: take the subway line no. 2. Exit on São Cristóvão station. Cross Quinta da Boa Vista park and take Parque Street going up until arriving at Fonseca Teles Street. Walk to the GESAR/UERJ building at number 121.   **train**: Exit on São Cristóvão station. All train lines stop at this station. Cross Quinta da Boa Vista park and take Parque Street going up until arriving at Fonseca Teles Street. Walk to the GESAR/UERJ building at number 121.   **on foot**: Go to Fonseca Teles Street, 121 - Sao Cristóvão, Rio de Janeiro.     ![s1]([[!!images/fonsecao.png]])
