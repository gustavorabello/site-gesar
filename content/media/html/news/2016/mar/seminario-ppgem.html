Seminário do PPG-EM UERJ da temporada 2016   **local**: Auditório da Incubadora Phoenix - Fonseca Teles 121, Rio de Janeiro - RJ; 3o. andar do edifício anexo. **data**: 14 de março de 2016 (segunda-feira) **horário**: 16h   ## NUMERICAL SEQUENCES IN SCIENTIFIC COMPUTING: Half Functional Analysis, half Philosophy look   **Autor**: Carlos A. de Moura - IME/UERJ - http://lattes.cnpq.br/7810980300080088   **Abstract**: The mathematicians real line is a deep theoretical concept. Its use in computer simulation is backed by the floating point "real" line theory, which has been incorporated to Numerical Analysis since its early days, by Wilkinson and von Neumann. Nevertheless, there exist still some blurred questions about computer simulation of real sequences convergence. These may either puzzle mathematical philosophy dwellers, like R. Hersh, or lead to some worries related to the very convergence proving. The latter ones are backed by Functional Analysis techniques, and may carry out the "false convergence" danger, as will be discussed.
