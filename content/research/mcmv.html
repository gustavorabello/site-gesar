To render the PMCMV's management even more effective, the GESAR group has been deploying a project which is intended to create a database system that able to support deeds for the immersion of the PMCMV program at the municipality of Queimados, in Rio de Janeiro state. Among the management tools made available are included: social registration of families, monitoring of works and infrastructure processes, and tracking of chronograms and financial administration. Besides, such tool proposes to expand older versions of the PMCMV's database in order to support government's decision makings throughout the federative levels. {%- endmark %} {% mark link-%} - [access database][1] [1]: {{'http://bcd-uerj.eng.br'}} - [read more][2] [2]: {{media_url('html/research/mcmv/text.html')}} {%- endmark %}
